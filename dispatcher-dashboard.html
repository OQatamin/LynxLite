Basic Dispatcher Features (Fleet Management Lite)

  1. Trip Assignment & Scheduling: Assign vehicles and drivers to specific trips. Set trip details (origin, destination, waypoints, ETA). Reschedule or cancel trips when needed.
  2. Live Vehicle & Driver Tracking: Monitor vehicle location in real-time (via GPS). See current driver status (Available, On Trip, Idle, Offline). Track ETA and route progress.
  3. Route Planning (Basic): Create and assign optimized routes. View suggested routes and traffic updates (if integrated). Allow rerouting in emergencies.
  4. Trip Status Monitoring: View trip lifecycle (Assigned → En Route → Completed). Get alerted if trips are delayed or deviated. Mark trips as completed if needed.
  5. Alert Management: Receive alerts from drivers (e.g., breakdowns, delays). Notify drivers of urgent changes or reroutes. Escalate unresolved issues to Fleet Manager.
  6. Driver Availability Overview: See which drivers are on duty, on break, on trip, or offline. View each driver’s recent activity and assignments.
  7. Load & Cargo Assignment (Optional): Assign cargo or delivery loads to vehicles (in logistics setups). Record cargo type, quantity, and special notes.
  8. Communication Panel: Send messages or instructions to drivers. Receive confirmations or issues from drivers. Central log of all communication (chat or notification).
  9. Trip Logs & Summary Reports (Lite View): Access recent trip records. Download basic reports for shift handover or internal review.
